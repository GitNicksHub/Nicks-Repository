Fishing

My opinion

I wholeheartedly believe that everyone should do whatever they love to do, of course, in addition to what must be done to survive. People need to rest from time to time in order to restore the energy spent on life. Otherwise, there is a high risk of having various physical and mental illnesses. Whether we call it a hobby, a favorite profession or a favorite job, the most important thing is that a person should be able to enjoy one hundred percent of what he does. Only then, will a person's love for life and striving for his goals increase, and he will be able to overcome all difficulties in order to achieve them. Why is it beneficial for health?

There are two types of fishing: lake and river. I like river fishing more. Fresh morning air, the beauty of nature, the sound of the river, the birds that come to drink the water and their beautiful sounds of singing; all of these make me feel like I am moving from one world to another. Also, since I move all the time, especially while doing the river fishing, it strengthens my health. What's more, constantly looking at the movement of water and green nature improves a person's eyesight. And naturally, such a rest is very good for the heart. Another important thing that should not be overlooked is of course the fish. Fish is very beneficial for health as a source of protein and vitamins.
